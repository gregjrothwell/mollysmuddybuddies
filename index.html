About Molly

Hello! I'm Molly, and I've turned my passion for dogs into a career I absolutely love. Every day, I get to explore the beautiful countryside with some incredible four-legged companions.

Whether your pup prefers a peaceful solo stroll or loves the energy of a group adventure, I tailor every walk to suit their personality and needs. Your dog's happiness and safety are my top priorities.

Our Services

From solo adventures to group fun, we've got every pup covered

Solo Walks

One-on-one attention for dogs who prefer a quieter adventure or need individual care. Perfect for anxious pups or those who just love having you all to themselves.

Group Walks

Fun social packs for playful, well-socialised dogs who love making friends. Small groups ensure every dog gets attention while enjoying the company.

Pet Sitting & Comfort Breaks

Home visits, feeding, and potty breaks while you're away. Whether you're at work or on holiday, your pet stays comfortable in familiar surroundings.
